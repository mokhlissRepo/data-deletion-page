User Data Deletion Instructions for [Your App Name]

We respect your privacy and provide two methods for deleting the data associated with your Meta (Facebook/Instagram) accounts from our application.

Method 1: Deleting within the Meta Ecosystem

You can remove our application's access entirely from your Facebook Account settings:

  1. Go to your Facebook **Settings & Privacy**.
  2. Select **Settings**.
  3. Go to **Apps and Websites**.
  4. Find and select **[Your App Name]**.
  5. Click **Remove**.

Removing the app will automatically trigger the deletion of all your stored data and tokens from our system.

Method 2: Direct Request

If you prefer, you can submit a direct request for data deletion:

  • **Email:** Please send an email to **support@[yourdomain.com]** with the subject line **"REQUEST FOR DATA DELETION"**.
  • **Required Information:** Include your **Facebook User ID** (optional, but helpful) and the email address associated with your **[Your App Name]** account.

We will confirm the deletion of all stored data (including access tokens, posts, and feedback) within 7 business days.
